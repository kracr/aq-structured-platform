Description

This ontology is an attempt at a ODP designed to model Pollution. Pollution can be defined as "Disequilibrium condition from equilibrium condition in any system".

Competency Questions

  1. What parameters are responsible for pollution?
  2. What is the time and place at which pollution was observed?
  3. What is the trajectory based contributor to the pollution?
  4. What are the attributes associated with a trajectory?
  5. What are the biological or chemical pollutants in the pollution?
